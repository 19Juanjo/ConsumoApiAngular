Last Man Standing

Last Man Standing is a comedy TV series about Mike Baxter, a traditional and outspoken man who works as the marketing director for an outdoor sporting goods store.The show follows Mike as he tries to maintain his values and perspective while living in a house with his wife and their three daughters, who often have very different opinions and lifestyles.It focuses on family life, generational differences, and everyday situations, using humor to show the challenges of balancing work, family, and personal beliefs.
